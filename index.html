About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at The University of Texas at Dallas, advised by Dr. Tien N. Nguyen. My research focuses on AI-assisted software engineering and testing (like fuzzing and web automation), with a particular emphasis on Large Language Models for improving software reliability. I am currently working as an AI Researcher in collaboration with DMI (Digital Management, LLC), where I develop production-scale NLP and LLM-based systems for automated compliance evaluation and AI-assisted code migration, bridging academic research with real-world deployment.
